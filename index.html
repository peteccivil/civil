MEET BVISOR

Bvisor is a civil engineering startup by the Department of Civil Engineering, PET Engineering College. We bridge the gap between academia and industry, delivering professional-grade solutions for global infrastructure projects.

Operating in partnership with Al Mussiver Structural Consultants, we combine faculty expertise and modern tools to ensure excellence and real-world training for young engineers.

Programme Educational Objectives

01

Professional Career

Gain knowledge and skills in Civil engineering to enable a career and professional accomplishment in the public or private sector organizations.

02

Expert Consultancy

Become consultants on complex real life Civil Engineering problems related to Infrastructure development especially housing and transport.

03

Entrepreneurship

Become entrepreneurs and develop processes to meet infrastructure needs with solutions that are technically sound and economically feasible.

04

Modern Research

Perform investigation for solving Civil Engineering problems by conducting research using modern equipment and software tools.

05

Multi-disciplinary

Function in multi-disciplinary teams and advocate policies, systems, and processes to support civil engineering.
